Use of 5G in Advanced Farming

Internet has been used in smart technology used in farms to produce high and better quality of crops. It even increase the farm yield many times. And since the department of Telecommunication has announced the arrival of 5G in India in 2022, it is going to have a positive impact on farming too.
Before going further let's know how internet helps farming chain. Few uses are as follows:-

(1) There are many agritech startups blooming in our beautiful country of India that use various mobile apps that connects farmers to customer. Major two of them are- Gurugram based otipy and Chenni based agritech B2C supply chain plateform Veg Route, both started in 2020 and are going very smoothly.

(2) Through internet farmers are able to do various online courses offered by the agricultural institutes of India (Kisan Seva Kendra) in order to increase their yield and have taken more advantages.

(3) The new technology helped farmers to buy any machinery related to farming with the help of internet and can even give feedback to these companies. Now with this new high end technology of 5G internet, farmers' production is going to increase for sure. AS the farmers are the backbone of the Indian economy, the backbone is going to be strengthen even more.

(4) Farmers are able to take benefit of various schemes of the government whether it be poultry farming subsidy, diary farming, fish farming etc. And here internet helps us a lot. India ha about 100 million farmers and hence development in this sector is very vital. finally its a boon for the farmers if they use technology traditional farming and will get benifited for sure.
